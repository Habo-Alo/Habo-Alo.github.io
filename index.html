My Story with Web Development

When I was in high school, I always wanted to be a teacher. When I started college, I began using computers and the internet more in my daily life.

That curiosity made me want to understand how computers, software, and web pages work. It motivated me to study computer information systems and start learning how to code.

I discovered that I enjoy hands-on projects and learning by building instead of only reading or watching videos. With Refcode, I am learning how to build my first website and improve my coding skills.
